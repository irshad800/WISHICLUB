Application and Membership FAQs

What is the registration fee for the Wish Island Club?

The registration fee is $50.

How can I complete the registration process?

You can complete the registration process by filling out the form online or at the office. The form must be submitted along with the registration fee.

What are the payment options for the registration fee?

The registration fee can be paid online, via bank transfer, or in person at the office.

How do I select a club package after registration?

After completing the registration, you can select your desired club package. Details on the available packages will be provided during the registration process.

When is the payment for the selected package due?

Payment for your selected package must be completed before or on 2026.

What are the payment methods for the club package?

Payment for the club package can be made online, via bank transfer, or at the office.

When will my membership benefits start?

Membership benefits will start on January 1, 2027.

Will I receive any information on how to access my benefits?

Yes, after your membership benefits start, you will receive a guide detailing how to access and use your benefits.
