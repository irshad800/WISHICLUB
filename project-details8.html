Membership Enrollment Process For Wish Island Club

1. Registration

Registration Fee: $50

Procedure: Complete the registration form on the Wish Island Club official website or at the club office. Submit the form along with the $50 registration fee via online payment, bank transfer, or in-office payment.

2. Plan Selection and Card Issuance

Plan Selection: After registration, members can select their desired club package.

Card Issuance: A membership card will be issued with details of the chosen package.

3. Payment for Package

Due Date: January 1, 2026

Details: Members must complete payment for their selected package by this date via online transactions, bank transfers, or in-person payments at the club office. A payment confirmation will be provided.

4. Commencement of Membership Benefits

Start Date: January 1, 2027

Details: Membership benefits will become active on this date. Members will receive a guide on how to access and utilize their benefits.


Benefits

Enjoy exclusive private check-in/out, 24/7 butler services, 15 luxurious suite nights annually at Wish Resort Island, and customized room settings tailored to your preferences.

Indulge in private beach yoga, bespoke spa treatments, chef’s tasting menus featuring fresh local ingredients, personalized gifts, and special loyalty benefits.

Beneficiaries

  • Primary Member: The individual who purchases the membership.

  • Family Members: Spouse, children, and immediate family members.


Wish Coins Benifits

Receive 100 coins
  • Exclusive Merchandise: Use Wish Coins to purchase exclusive Wish Island branded merchandise such as beachwear, hats, eco-friendly tote bags, or spa kits.
  • Gift Vouchers: Exchange Wish Coins for gift vouchers to share with friends or family (e.g., $50 voucher for 5,000 Coins).
  • Kids’ Club Perks: Complimentary kids’ club passes or priority access to special kids’ activities with Wish Coins.
  • Photo Memories: Use Wish Coins for professional photo-shoots during your stay to capture special moments.
  • Private Dining Experiences: Redeem Coins for exclusive candlelight dinners or beachside BBQ experiences tailored just for you.
  • Charity Contributions: Donate Wish Coins to resort-supported sustainability initiatives or local community projects.

Cancellation Policies

Refund: No refunds for early cancellation; benefits expire if membership is not renewed.
