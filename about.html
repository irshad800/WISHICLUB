Escape to Wish Island Club’s exclusive private beaches, where luxury meets tranquility. Relax on soft sands, savor romantic seaside dining, or unwind in a cabana as waves set the perfect ambiance.

Club History

Founded in 2024, Wish Island Club offers unparalleled luxury and exclusivity in the breathtaking Maldives, creating a sanctuary where members escape the ordinary and embrace the extraordinary.

  • Our Vision

    To be the most exclusive and sought-after membership club in the Maldives, offering unparalleled luxury, experiences, and personalized services.

  • Our Mission

    Our mission is to deliver unique, bespoke experiences with exclusive events, world-class accommodations, and personalized services, all while championing sustainability and social responsibility.
